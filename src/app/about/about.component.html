About

My goal is to help people explore attractions in the city they are looking forward to visit. However, weather can ruins the trip if one is not aware of it. This Website allows the user to view the climate conditions and the attractions of that city. Furthermore, helps them smartly plan the day ahead without getting is ruined by Climate Conditions

Working

  • Click on More info on weather card to see the hourly update of the day
  • Click on Less info on weather card to hide the info
  • City input box => Type the name of the city such as Vancouver, New York, Agra and so on
  • On Country Abbrevation input box => Type Country's Abbreviation such as CA, US, IN and so on
  • The search button will stay disabled until both the input has been filled
  • If the input has wrong city or abbreviation, there will be no Attraction cards displayed at the bottom
  • On Attractions panel scroll left or right to see more attractions
  • Click on more info on the attraction cards to see detailed information about them
